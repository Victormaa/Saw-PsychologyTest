"Color Correction Pro" compatible with Unity 2019 and Cinemachine.
"Color Correction Pro" is designed for more professional adjustment of camera effects in your projects and easy to use.

"Color Correction Pro" allows you to customize:
  ▪ Hue, saturation, lightness, brightness, contrast, gamma, sharpness, temperature, threshold  
  ▪ Color balance
  ▪ RGB channels, in the form of curves
  ▪ Input and output levels  
  ▪ Photo filter
  ▪ LUT filter
  ▪ Ramp filter

In the LUTs and Ramps directories, there are textures for the LUT and Ramp filters.
24 LUT textures and 20 Ramp textures.

To use "Color Correction Pro", drag and drop "ColorCorrectionPro.cs" script to a camera 
or click "Add Component" -> "Image Effects" -> "Color Adjustments" -> "Color Correction Pro".
